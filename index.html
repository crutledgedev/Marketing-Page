Our Mission

In 2015, an estimated 150 million people worldwide were homeless. Our mission is to provide a reliable network of communication with a single point destination. Connecting social workers, humanitarian aid providers and the communities they serve with the families of those who may be searching for each other, regardless of organization, affiliation or geographic location.

Our Volunteers

Our volunteers are actively involved with organizations that provide community outreach, humanitarian relief and access to critical care services to homeless communities in their local regions.

From Social Workers, Church volunteers, soup-kitchen workers and all points in between. Volunteers are the contact point bridging the gap, posting new alerts and receiving messages when a response or post is submitted.
